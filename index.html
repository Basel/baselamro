#4Podcast

Podcast geek


Podcast has always been my gateway to feed my curiosity and escape to another world. I listen mainly to science, psychology and stories shows. I was asked several times to recommend podcasts (Especially in this strange period). So here is a list of my top shows:


    Radiolab: is an investigation told through sounds and stories, and centered around one big idea.

    Hidden Brain: uses science and storytelling to reveal the unconscious patterns that drive human behavior, shape our choices and direct our relationships.

    Death, Sex & Money is a podcast about the big questions and hard choices that are often left out of polite conversation.

    Invisibilia: Unseeable forces control human behavior and shape our ideas, beliefs, and assumptions. The show fuses narrative storytelling with science that will make you see your own life differently.

    Ted Radio Hour: Exploring the biggest questions of our time with the help of the world's greatest thinkers. Host Manoush Zomorodi inspires us to learn more about the world, our communities, and most importantly, ourselves.

    The Moth: The Moth has presented thousands of true stories, told live and without notes, to standing-room-only crowds worldwide.

    Throughline: The past is never past. Every headline has a history. The show goes back in time to understand the present. These are stories you can feel and sounds you can see from the moments that shaped our world.
